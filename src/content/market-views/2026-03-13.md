## Global Cross-Asset Snapshot
The weekly cross-asset mix frames how Vietnam risk should be priced. A softer DXY usually helps EM equities, but the benefit is incomplete if USD/VND does not follow. Lower WTI helps Vietnam as a net energy importer, but an oil spike quickly becomes an inflation and margin issue. Gold and BTC help identify whether investors are buying hedges, speculative risk, or neither.

In this week, the VN-Index reaction was therefore not just a local technical move. It sat inside a global tape where dollar direction, oil volatility, and foreign investor behavior all mattered for near-term risk tolerance.
